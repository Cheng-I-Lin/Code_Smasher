General Instructions:
There is a total of 5 different games modes you can choose, each with a different way to play. To start, click on the shapes corresponding to the titles of the game. To play the game, use the keyboard controls on your computer and the mouse and follow the corresponding instructions for each game mode. By clicking the "BACK" button on the screen, the game will end and the screen will be transitioned back the title/introduction screen.

Square Up:
Square Up is a two player game where each player would use either the arrow keys or the W, A, S, and D keys to control the movements of a square. Each player can move the square left to right and can also make it jump. Moreover, by pressing either the S key or the down arrow key, the square will shrink, becoming a smaller square with a faster movement speed. The objective of this game is to try to jump on top of the other player's square, and the first to do so wins
